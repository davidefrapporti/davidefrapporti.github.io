In October 2021, I was awarded the qualification of "Privatdozent" through the “Lehrbefugnis für das Fachgebiet Mathematik”.


Research interests:

My field of research is complex algebraic geometry, in particular surfaces and varieties of general type and related topics. I am mostly interested in the construction (and classification) of new surfaces, in the study of their geometrical properties, and in the construction of new varieties having special properties. I mainly use a combinatorial and computational approach involving tools from topology, group theory, and representation theory and the aid of a computer algebra system.
